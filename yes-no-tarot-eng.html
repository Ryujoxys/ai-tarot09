How to Perform a Yes/No Tarot Reading?

Prepare Your Mind

In a quiet environment, take deep breaths and relax. Meditate on the yes/no question you want to solve, allowing your inner self to resonate with the tarot cards. This step helps improve the accuracy of your yes/no tarot reading.

Choose a Yes/No Spread

Yes/No tarot readings typically use either a single card or three-card spread. A single card directly answers your yes/no question with concise clarity; a three-card spread provides more detailed background information and guidance.

Ask a Clear Question

Ensure your yes/no question is clear and specific. For example, "Is this job right for me?" or "Is now a good time to start a new relationship?" Avoid vague questions to get accurate yes/no tarot reading results.
