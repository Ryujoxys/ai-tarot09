Frequently Asked Questions About AI Tarot Reading

Are AI Tarot readings effective?

AI Tarot reading combines traditional tarot card systems with advanced artificial intelligence technology. Through big data analysis and pattern recognition, it provides interpretations with reference value. While it cannot predict the future with 100% accuracy, it can help you think about issues from different perspectives and gain valuable insights.

How can I improve the accuracy of AI Tarot readings?

Ask specific and clear questions, maintain an open mind, and understand the meanings of tarot cards in relation to your own situation during the interpretation process. This way, you can obtain more accurate and valuable reading results.

Can Tarot cards predict time?

AI Tarot reading can provide a general timeframe for event development, but accurate time predictions are influenced by many factors. Tarot cards focus more on revealing the development trends and possible outcomes of events rather than precise timing.

Do Tarot readings reflect inevitable outcomes?

Tarot cards show possible outcomes based on current energies and trends, not unchangeable destiny. You always have free will and can make wiser choices based on the guidance of tarot cards, thereby changing the course of your future.

How long should I wait before asking the same question again?

It is generally recommended to wait 2-4 weeks before doing another reading on the same question, giving enough time for the situation to develop. Frequent readings on the same question may lead to confusing interpretations unless there have been significant changes. Premium members can schedule readings more flexibly according to their needs.
