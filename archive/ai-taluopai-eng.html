AI Tarot Reading FAQs

Is AI Tarot Reading effective?

AI Tarot Reading combines traditional tarot systems with advanced artificial intelligence technology, providing valuable interpretations through big data analysis and pattern recognition. While it cannot predict the future with 100% accuracy, it can help you think about issues from different perspectives and gain beneficial insights.

How to improve the accuracy of AI Tarot Reading?

Ask specific and clear questions, maintain an open mindset, and understand the meanings of tarot cards in conjunction with your actual situation during the interpretation process. This way you can get more accurate and valuable reading results.

Can tarot cards predict timing?

AI Tarot Reading can provide a general timeframe for event development, but accurate time predictions are influenced by multiple factors. Tarot cards focus more on revealing the development trends and possible outcomes of events rather than precise timing.

Do tarot readings reflect inevitable results?

Tarot cards show possible outcomes based on current energies and trends, not unchangeable destiny. You always have free will and can make wiser choices based on tarot guidance, thereby changing your future path.

How long should I wait before asking the same question again?

It's generally recommended to wait 2-4 weeks before asking the same question again, giving enough time for the situation to develop. Frequent readings on the same question may lead to confused interpretations, unless there are obvious changes in the situation.
